Famous Asian Canadian: Dr. Theresa Tam

Short introduction:

Dr. Theresa Tam is one of the Canadian public health officer of Canada, whom we often see on TV on news channel. She was offically appointed to be the public health officer of Canada on June 26, 2017 after the former officer, Gregory Talyor, retired on Dec 16, 2016. COVID-19 pandemic:

COVID-19, one of the most severe and influencial disease in the world. Started in Wuhan, China in a seafood market and soon it spreadOn March, 2020 Dr. Tam began making announcements about social distancing and personal hygiene. It was also the month that WHO declared COVID-19 to be a global pandemic. The first case of COVID-19 entered Canada on Jan 27, 2020 after an individual returned to Toronto from Wuhan and tested positive. On April 23, Dr. Tam was appointed by Justin Trudeau to form a new advistory body to defend the pandemic.
